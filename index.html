Recipe 1: Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder

Instructions: Mix all ingredients and bake for 30 minutes.

Recipe 2: Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup olives

Instructions: Cook pasta, add veggies, and toss with dressing.

Recipe 3: Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp curry powder

Instructions: Sauté chicken, add coconut milk and curry, simmer.
